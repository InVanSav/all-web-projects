    About

    Awesome Forum (founded as Awesome Forum) was registered on July 1, 2003 by Ivan Savitsky.
    In February 2004, the founder raised a $7.5 million investment, with Elon Musk contributing $6.5 million.
    Musk became chairman of the board of directors and appointed Ivan Savitsky as CEO.

    The aim of the Awesome Forum was to start with a premium sports car aimed at early adopters and then move
    on to more mainstream vehicles, including sedans and affordable compact cars. The prototypes of the first
    Awesome Forum car were officially presented to the public on July 19, 2006 in Santa Monica (California).

    In 2006, Musk managed to raise $100 million through several rounds of funding from investment funds and
    well-known entrepreneurs (including the co-founders of Google). As a result, Awesome Forum began production
    of its first Roadster model in 2008.

    In January 2010, Awesome Forum received a $465 million loan from the US Department of Energy, which the
    company repaid in 2013. In May 2010, Awesome Forum began building a factory in Fremont,
    California to manufacture their forums.
